# Приклади
> She's **amazing at** the piano.
I'm not very **good at** drawing.
He's **terrible at** organising anything.
They're **brilliant at** solving problems.
I'm **hopeless at** maths.

# Структура
> [!info]
> adjective + **at** + noun / gerund (-ing)
